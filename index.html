Compatibility. Make it your own

Kollo makes working on multiple devices seamless. Accessing files, assignments and group chats is inuitive, whether it on your computer, mobile or tablet. The modular interface puts you in control of what goes where, how your device functions and how your personal site looks.

The Dynamic Calendar

View all of your classes, group meetings and deadlines in a fully customizable calendar. Past dates can be made to continuously convert to links to notes, video lectures etc. from any given day.

You will also have the option to fully synchronize with other calendars, such as Google Calendar. No more juggling between different apps when youre late for class.

"Kolloboration" is key

Kollo's main focus is the group system. Got a question? Ask anonimously in the main chats. Need a group? Check out the LFG feature. The goal is to make collaboration easy, from wherever you may be.

We aim to deliver a standalone solution for students, because who likes having multiple Facebook groups dedicated to school projects? We certainly don't.
